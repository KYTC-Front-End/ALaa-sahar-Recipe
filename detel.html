COOK TIME

40 Min

SERVINGS

40 Min

CALORIES

294kcal

Ingredients:

500 gm minced meat

A teaspoon of garlic powder, salt and pepper

Burger cheese slices

Lettuce leaf cut into slices

A large tomato slice, cut into slices

Toasted burger bread

How to prepare:

Step 1

Put the dried mushroom slices into the kebbeh, until they are completely ground, and remove any large mushroom pieces that were not ground.

Step 2

In a large bowl, use your clean hands to gently mix the meat, salt, ground mushrooms, black pepper, and Worcestershire sauce, mixing until combined, 1 or 2 minutes..

Step 3

Shape the meat into 3-4 circles like pancakes, Preheat a large skillet over high heat for 1 minute, add fresh mushrooms and sauté until dry, 2-3 minutes. .

Step 4

Add the onions and olive oil, stir and continue to sauté over high heat for 1 minute. Add salt to taste and cook until the onions are soft and beginning to brown. Turn off the heat and place the mixture in a bowl.
